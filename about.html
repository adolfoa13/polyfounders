OUR MISSION:

Our story began in 2012, Poly Founders was created with the purpose of connecting young entrepreneurs of all backgrounds under a common roof. While Cal Poly Pomona is our home, we welcome students, alumni, and professionals from all universities and organizations. Poly Founders works with budding entrepreneurs, exceptional students, and prominent professors in order to unite the worlds of entrepreneurship, academia, and industry. We help entrepreneurs by offering our resources, knowledge, and network.

EXECUTIVE BOARD:

The Poly Founders E-board is a multidisciplinary group of students varying from Finance to Computer Engineering. Students run every year for their desired position.
